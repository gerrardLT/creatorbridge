**Q: How do you handle gas fees?**
> "We use Coinbase Smart Wallet with paymaster integration, which means creators can interact with the blockchain without holding native tokens. For high-volume users, we're exploring batch transactions and Layer 2 scaling."

**Q: What about legal enforceability?**
> "Story Protocol licenses are designed to be legally binding. We're working with legal oracles to bridge on-chain licenses with traditional legal systems. Additionally, the immutable record serves as evidence in dispute resolution."

**Q: How does derivative tracking work technically?**
> "When a derivative is registered, we call Story Protocol's `registerDerivative` function, which creates an on-chain relationship graph. Smart contracts automatically calculate and distribute revenue shares based on the parent IP's license terms."
